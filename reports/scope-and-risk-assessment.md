# Scope, Goals, and Risk Assessment

## Scope
The scope of this internal audit covers the entire security program at Botium Toys, including IT assets, systems, networks, and data.

## Goals
- Assess current IT assets
- Identify risks and vulnerabilities
- Evaluate security controls and compliance gaps

## Risk Assessment Summary
- **Risk Score:** 8/10
- **Primary Risk:** Inadequate asset management and missing security controls
- **Impact Level:** Medium to High
